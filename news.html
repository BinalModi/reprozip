What's New

  • ReproZip is listed on the Artifact Evaluation Process guidelines.
  • ReproZip v1.0.2 has been released.
  • ReproZip is the recommended tool for the Reproducibility Section of the Information Systems Journal, Elsevier.
  • ReproZip is the recommended tool for the ACM SIGMOD 2015 Reproducibility Review.
